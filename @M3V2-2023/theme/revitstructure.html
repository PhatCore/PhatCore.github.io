SOME NOTES & RULES

1 - Modelling 3D workflow

* Modelling is needed to confirm whether to use revit model to transfer other analysis software? Based on the complex level of building such as curved, diagal, and continuous curvilinear structure, it is necessary to carefully and thoroughly model the analytical link in revit.
* Confirm to deploy 2D or 3D rebar (depend on clients)
* Always update the customer's architectural model with the structural model to update and handle the model as well as it is important to change the structural methods according to the architecture if necessary


2 - Deploy drawings workflow

* Title Block Template (A3/A0...)
* View Template (To be applied to drag on sheets) => need to confirm manager
* Crop view (often) or Scope of box (to be used to divide smaller plan)
* Structure Plan and Title Plan Family-according to client's commnents
* Alive section or Dummy section
* Details/ Call out (Connections 2D or 3D)
* Line Style, Text Style, Work Line
